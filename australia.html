Replay your Melbourne, Australia Trip

I only stayed a week over in Melbourne. This was the best trip of the year by far.

The Big Wedding

The main reason I went here. To see my good friend get married.

In the City

As soon as we landed, we explored. Our AirBnB was in the city, so it was easy to go around.

Zoo and the Beaches

The newlyweds and the in-laws took us to the zoo to see kangaroos. Then we headed to the beach. The beach was beautiful and clear.

Making Friends

I met a lot of good people over there. They took us to hang out and play basketball. We had a blast!
